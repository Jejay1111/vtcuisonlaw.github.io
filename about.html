US Law Practice

Attorney Cuison is currently practicing U.S. Immigration Law, with major emphasis on the following:

  • Family-based immigration (including VAWA Self-Petition and Waiver Applications)
  • Employment-based immigration (with focus on EB-2, EB-3 & EB-3 Other Workers, & Permanent Labor Certification [PERM])
  • Non-immigrant visa petitions (including H-1B, F-1/M-1, K-1/K-3, B1/B2)
  • Application for naturalization and certificate of citizenship
  • Removal/deportation defense and asylum on a case-by-case basis
